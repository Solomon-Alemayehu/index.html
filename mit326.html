MIT326101-Electronic Medical Course Overview

This course covers the theories and principles that are fundamental for understanding in order to be able to navigate, design and implement Electronic Health and Electronic Medical Records. There is no required textbook, all materials will be provided on slides and handouts. I will post slides the weeks that we’re meeting and will discuss in further detail during lecture, you are strongly encouraged to attend class as participation impacts your grade.

The course is NOT a hands-on approach to developing medical records, there are numerous vendors i.e. Epic, Cerner, Meditech etc. and each contains anywhere from 20-50 different modules i.e. Pharmacy, Lab, Radiology. You will learn each module including registration, billing, and interfacing.
